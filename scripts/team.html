TEAM STRUCTURE

subdivisions in the team

Departments

Powertrain

The primary objective was to enhance the responsiveness of the powertrain in order to improve drivability. This department deals with the motors and transmission transfer of the power from motor to wheel with the help of gears , shafts etc . Department focus on tuning of the engine , along with designing and manufacturing of highly tuned intake and exhaust manifolds for increased output in mid RPM range ,Programmable ECU , customized cooling to ensure optimal running of the engine .

Vehicle Dynamics

vehicle dynamics deals with the suspension geometry along with steering geometry and the wheel assembly of the car .The primary objective of the design was to develop a highly tunable and robust system and track tune it to accommodate the requirements of the driver and the powertrain. A tremendous reduction in un-sprung mass without compromising structural integrity was the secondary aim.

Aerodynamics

Aerodynamics deals with designing of a front wing , rear wing , diffuser, nose and sidepods alongside of the car. The designing of the aerodynamics focused primarily on maximizing downforce while minimizing the associated drag penalty and centred around reliability, low weight, manufacturing feasibility while ensuring appreciable aesthetics

Chassis

The objective of the chassis design was to make the lightest possible rule compliant chassis while maximizing torsional rigidity and achieving a compact yet ergonomic structure.

Electronics & Software

The primary objective of the electronics department is to facilitate the fine-tuning of the car’s setup by providing real-time track data and develop an interactive driver modeling and training platform. In addition to the aforementioned, the department also aims to design and fabricate a reliable electrical power management system for the vehicle.

Drive Train

The prime focus was to minimize weight, optimize final drive ratio and torque bias. The Drexler FSAE Salisbury clutch pack differential weighs less and performs better compared to the previous Torsen Differential. shafts were introduced minimizing rotational inertia. A chain tensioning mechanism using stainless steel spacers lead to a simpler design and weight reduction. An electronic gear shifter actuated via steering mounted buttons ensures better ergonomics, faster shifting hence improved lap times.

Management

It consist of three departments:

Corporate : department focus on geeting financial support and in kind sponsors throughout the season.

Administration : department maintains communication bridge between university and team . Department focus on making travel and meeting arrangements, preparing reports and maintaining appropriate filing systems to fulfill the necessary requirements of the team .

Finance and Logistics : this department deals with day to day transactions , keeping the account of the team’s finances. Reimbursement of the bills for the various parts and tools . The logistics deals in making arrangements for the amenities required in the workshop .
