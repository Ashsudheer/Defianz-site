Team Defianz Racing

Team Defianz Racing is the Formula Student team of Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering). It was founded in 2003 with a vision to inculcate learning and innovation among budding engineers. The team, undisputedly, has Asia’s best and longest-lived legacy. We were Asia’s first team to venture into this project and have brought glory and recognition to the nation on several occasions by winning numerous accolades.
